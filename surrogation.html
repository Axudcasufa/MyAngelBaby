What is Surrogation?

Surrogation is a legal arrangement where a woman carries and gives birth to a child for another person or couple who cannot conceive or carry a pregnancy themselves.

Surrogacy can be a wonderful option for those facing infertility, but it also involves complex legal and emotional considerations.
